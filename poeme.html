Mon poeme

In your eyes, I see the sunrise, A warmth that melts the night away. You hold my hand, and in that touch, I feel the world shift and sway.

Your laughter, like music, fills my soul, A melody that guides me home. In every glance, a thousand words, No need for silence, no need to roam.

Through your smile, I find my peace, A quiet love that never fades. With you, the storm becomes a breeze, And within your heart is where my cares are set.

Together we build up our tale, brick by brick, Into a world where dreams and hopes collide. Within you, I find strength to grow, My love for you, an endless tide. .
